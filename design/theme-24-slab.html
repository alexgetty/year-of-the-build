Body Copy

This is the Year of the Build. Twelve months. Twelve projects. No excuses. The goal isn't perfection—it's momentum. Ship fast, learn faster, iterate always.

Each week, I'll document what I built, what broke, and what I learned. Raw, unfiltered progress updates that show the real work behind launching products. The ugly commits, the 2am debugging sessions, the "why did I think this was a good idea" moments.

This isn't a highlight reel. It's a construction log.
